# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 2026**

## About Rippl

Rippl is an offline-first Bluetooth and WiFi Direct mesh messenger. Messages are sent directly between devices without any servers or internet connection.

## Data We Collect

Rippl does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any server. We have no servers.

Everything stays on your device:
- Your display name
- Your contacts
- Your messages
- Your cryptographic identity

## Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:
- Your name or email address
- Your phone number
- Your location
- Your IP address
- Device identifiers
- Usage statistics or analytics
- Any message content

## Crash Reporting

Rippl does not currently use any crash reporting or analytics tools. No data is sent to any third party.

## Bluetooth and Location Permissions

Rippl requests Bluetooth and Location permissions solely to discover nearby devices and build the mesh network. This data is used only on your device and is never stored or transmitted anywhere.

## Nearby WiFi Devices Permission

Rippl requests the Nearby WiFi Devices permission on Android 13 and above to enable WiFi Direct mesh networking for longer range communication up to 200 meters. This data never leaves your device.

## Message Security

All messages are end-to-end encrypted using ECDH key agreement and AES-256-GCM encryption. Only the intended recipient can read your messages. Relay nodes in the mesh network only see encrypted data they cannot read.

## Your Identity

Your cryptographic identity (keypair) is generated on your device and never leaves it. You can export and back up your identity using a password you choose. We never have access to your keys.

## Data Deletion

To delete all your data simply uninstall Rippl. All messages, contacts, and identity data are permanently deleted from your device. We have no copies of your data.

## Third Party Services

Rippl does not integrate with any third party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## GDPR — European Users

Since Rippl collects no personal data on any server there is nothing for us to provide, modify, or delete beyond uninstalling the app from your device. Since Rippl does not collect, store, or transmit any sensitive personal data or information, the data protection obligations under these rules do not apply.
