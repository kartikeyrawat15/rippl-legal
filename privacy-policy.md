# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 2026**

## About Rippl

Rippl is an offline-first Bluetooth and WiFi Direct mesh messenger. Messages are sent directly between devices without any servers or internet connection.

## Data We Collect

Rippl does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any server. We have no servers.

Everything stays on your device:
- Your display name
- Your contacts
- Your messages
- Your cryptographic identity

## Crash Reporting

Rippl does not currently use any crash reporting or analytics tools. No data is sent to any third party.

## Bluetooth and Location Permissions

Rippl requests Bluetooth and Location permissions to discover nearby devices and build the mesh network. This data is never stored or transmitted outside your device.

## Message Security

All messages are end-to-end encrypted using ECDH key agreement and AES-256-GCM encryption. Only the intended recipient can read your messages. Relay nodes in the mesh network only see encrypted data they cannot read.

## Your Identity

Your cryptographic identity (keypair) is generated on your device and never leaves it. You can export and back up your identity using a password you choose. We never have access to your keys.

## Data Deletion

To delete all your data simply uninstall Rippl. All messages, contacts, and identity data are permanently deleted from your device.

## Children

Rippl is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
